About

Executive Overview

A practitioner-leader who moves between the clinical and the commercial without compromise.

I am, first and foremost, an International Education Consultant and Clinical Mental Health Professional — bridging South Asia and Australia through cross-border education leadership and Australian-trained psychosocial practice. Around this dual foundation, I've also built supporting ventures and additional expertise in English language and IELTS training, public event hosting, and digital entrepreneurship training (Amazon FBA, Shopify) — each extending, rather than competing with, the same core mission of cross-cultural impact and measurable outcomes.

My foundation is clinical: I hold a Graduate Certificate and Certificate IV in Mental Health from Australian institutions. I spent 3+ years delivering person-centered psychosocial support in Adelaide, managing complex caseloads, and coordinating multidisciplinary recovery pathways. I understand the nuances of trauma-informed care, human resilience, and therapeutic alliance.

My evolution is strategic: I transitioned to founding and leading international education consultancy and digital training enterprises. I've directed university partnerships, scaled English language programs, mentored 500+ students across continents, and built ventures that serve markets across Pakistan and internationally. I operate with operational discipline, financial acumen, and accountability for outcomes.
